The Series has Evolved in a BIG Way!

After the success of the 2022 Carolina Series Championships, we're excited to announce the next big step in the local Pokemon TCG scene! The "Carolina Series" has evolved into the South Eastern Series Championships! (SE Series for short.) Integrating other States into the series, beggining with Georgia and Tennessee!


Get ready to compete in events across multiple South Eastern States over the next several months, accumulate points towards an invitation to the bigger than ever, SE Grand Prix! Please check out the "About" page for further details, as a lot is changing!



The 2022 State Championships took place on July 31st, 2022. Spanning from January through June, more than 300 players competed in more than 90 events across the Carolinas, fighting for a invitation to the State Championships. The Top 32 players with the most points by the end of June earned an Invitation, plus the four players that attended the most events throughout the season. The series wrapped up with a Last Chance Qualifier the day prior to States. The Top 4 finishers of the LCQ earned a last minute spot to compete in States the next day! You can view the "Results" page to see which players made it through and what decks were played!
