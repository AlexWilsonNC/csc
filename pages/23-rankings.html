• Players that earn 100 or more SE Points earn an invitation to the SE Grand Prix.

• Players that earn 250 or more SE Points earn a Round-One bye at the SE Grand Prix.

• The Top 4 players in Attendance Points (that dont reach 100 SE Points) earn an Invitation to the SE Grand Prix.

* (Included any players with the same amount of Attendance Points as 4th place.)

• Players with at least 1 Attendance Point (that dont reach 100 SE Points) can compete in the SE Grand Prix's Last Chance Qualifier.

* (The top finishers of the LCQ get to compete in the SE Grand Prix the next day.)
