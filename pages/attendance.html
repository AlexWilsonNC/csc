• The Top 32 players in CS Points earn an Invitation to States. Including anyone with the same amount of CS Points as 32nd Place.

* (Top 8 tie-breakers decided by fewest Attendance Points)

• The Top 4 players in Attendance Points (that aren't in Top 32) earn an Invitation to the State Championships.

* (Any players with the same amount of Attendance Points as 4th included.)

• Players with at least 3 Attendance Points can compete in the Last Chance Qualifier on July 30th.
