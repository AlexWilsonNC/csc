Standard Format

Only cards with the regulation marks of either D, E and F are legal. (Any set or promo released since Sword & Shield)

Legal promo list: here

Player who goes first cannot attack, nor use a Supporter.

English cards only.

GLC Format

View the Gym Leader Challenge by Andrew Mahone

Any set from Black & White (2011) up through our recent sets in the Sword & Shield series are legal.

Only one of each card with the same name allowed per deck. Except for Basic Energy.

All Pokemon in deck must be of the same type. (ie. only Psychic Pokemon, only Grass Pokemon...)

Banned Cards: Ace Specs, Rule Box Pokemon (ie EXs, Vs, Prisms, Radiant cards...), Oranguru from Ultra Prism (Resource Managment), Lysandre's Trump Card, Forest of Giant Plants, Chip-Chip Ice Axe, & Hiker.

Decks consist of 60 cards, non-see-through sleeves.

Player who goes first cannot attack nor use a Supporter.

Foreign cards allowed w/ suitable reference.
