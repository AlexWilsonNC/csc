Get Ready for the 2022 State Championships!

This is it! Over 90 events from the last 6 months have all lead to this final event weekend!

Who will become the first ever "Carolina State Champion"!? Which 4 players will fight their way successfully through the Last Chance Qualifier? How many memories will be made...?! See you all soon, at the 2022 Carolina Series State Invitational Championships!


The entire weekend takes place in Concord, NC

8440 Pit Stop Ct NW, Concord NC

Mighty Meeple



Last Chance Qualifier

July 30

Registration: 11am - 12pm

Entry: $20

(Payment accepted at venue)

- Paper decklists REQUIRED -

- Deck checks WILL be conducted -

Entry Requirment:

Must have earned at least 3 Attendance Points from throughout the season

(Players without 3 Attendance Points will be denied entry)

Format: Standard (SSH - GO)

Best of 3 Rounds (50 minutes)

Going into cut, or after Top 8, the final four players move onto States the next day. There will be no need to play out any Top 4 of cut rounds, the LCQ simply ends with the announcment of the final 4 players standing.

The 4 Players that make it through the LCQ do not pay entry for States

Prizing: all LCQ particpants recieve a Limited Edition Carolina Series commemorative Dice

(There is no additional prizing for the LCQ, players are simply competing for the chance to play in the State Invitational Championships, with the Top 4 players earning that privilege!)

State Invitational Championships

July 31

Registration: 11am - 12pm

Entry: (All invitees have paid entry)

- Paper decklists REQUIRED -

- Deck checks WILL be conducted -

- Decklists MUST be submitted before 12pm -

Entry Requirment:

Must have earned an official Invitation from Alex Wilson

(Invitees come from being Top 32 in the CS Points Rankings, Top 4 in Attendance Points, and the Top 4 players from the LCQ - additional players will have been reached out to if some of the above are unable to make it out to the event, invited in order of CS Points Rankings from 33rd Place - down the line)

Format: Standard (SSH - GO)

Best of 3 Rounds (50 minutes)

Top 8: Best of 3 Rounds (75 minutes)

Prizing: all State Invitational particpants will recieve a Limited Edition Carolina Series commemorative Dice

(The 4 Players that made it through the LCQ will not recieve a second Dice)

1st Place Prizing:

  • Champion Trophy
  • Champion Playmat
  • Gold TCEvolutions VSTAR Counter
  • Gold TCEvolutions Dice Set
  • x4 Booster Boxes

  • Finalist Trophy
  • Finalist Playmat
  • Red TCEvolutions Dice Set
  • x3 Booster Boxes

    • Top 4 Prizing:

  • Semi-Finalist Trophy
  • Semi-Finalist Playmat
  • x2 Booster Boxes

  • Either half a Box or 1 Box each (TBD)
