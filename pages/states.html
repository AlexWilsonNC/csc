The 2022 State Championships took place on July 31st, 2022. Spanning from January through June, more than 300 players competed in more than 90 events across the Carolinas, fighting for a invitation to the State Championships. The Top 32 players with the most points by the end of June earned an Invitation, plus the four players that attended the most events throughout the season. The series wrapped up with a Last Chance Qualifier the day prior to States. The Top 4 finishers of the LCQ earned a last minute spot to compete in States the next day!


Chris Urcinola - State Champion

Chris piloted an Arceus Duraludon deck, Skyscraper-ing his way through all four Top Cut rounds becoming the first ever Carolina Series State Champion! Be on the lookout for this accomplished player bearing his Champion playmat.

Caleb Rogerson - Finalist

Caleb, a multi-time Regional & International champion, dominated the Carolina Rankings all season! Arguably bringing the best deck to the event, but ultimately unable to take down Duraludon VMAX.

Grant Manley - Semi-Finalist

One of the best players in the World, and #1 in the Carolina Rankings in the end, it was no surprise to see him make it this far! He and Caleb had a friendly rivalry between the 1st place ranking throughout the year.

James Briglia - Semi-Finalist

Like Caleb and Grant above, James earned a Top 8 invite based on his awesome accomplishments throughout the season, a well-deserved Top 4 finish by this Dark-type enthusiast!
